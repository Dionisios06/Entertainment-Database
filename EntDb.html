    • Importance In The Franchise (IITF)
  • 🌕 = Mainline: the ones everyone aware of the franchise's existance is aware of &/or are needed to understand the basic story.
  • 🌔 = Technically Mainline: it's complicated, think of the Star Wars movies/episodes VII, VIII & IX.
  • 🌓 = Secondery: the ones that most of the fandom knows about (mostly spin-offs).
  • 🌒 = Alternative: an edited version of a piece of the franchise (like a series edited to a movie with maybe a few extra scenes) or an unpopular remake.
  • 🌑 = Bottom Of The Barrel: only for hyperfans & the ones that have been starved for years without a new entry.
    • Extra
  • #### = Put in places of unknown numbers.
  • ◯ = Put in the "quantity" cells (when the media isn't a "series") that has only one released product.
  • † = Put in the "quantity" cells (after the default text) for media that have been cancelled in the middle of production or for cancelled games that had DEMOs/early accesses released.
  • © = Put in the "year" cells (above the default text) for media that are in the "Public Domain".
  • 🤖 = Put in the "media" cells (after the default text) for media that have even the smallest use of A.I. in its final product (for something that is meant to be seen by the consumer, behind the scene & prototype stuff don't count).
    • Rules
  • If the English translation of a word from a language with an oficial Latin script is just the same without the diacritics (usually in names) then they will be added right back.
  • When a show doesn't have unique names for its seasons/arcs, their number will be added next to the title in parenthesis both in the original language & in English. If the title is the same in both the English & original title, then both will be written separately.
  • Games have in their "year" cell the year of its full release, not of the DEMO/early access unless it was cancelled (which would lead to having to add a † in the corresponding "quantity" cell).
